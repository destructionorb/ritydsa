RIT YDSA ENDORSES BERNIE SANDERS FOR US PRESIDENT - JANUARY 3RD, 2020

Rochester Institute of Technology's chapter of the Young Democratic Socialists of America endorses candidate Bernie Sanders for election in this year's US presidential race. Sanders' long-running commitment to compassionate justice serves as a preeminent example of socialist action. RIT YDSA stands with Senator Sanders as we prepare for the upcoming Iowa Caucus.

Sanders' consistent support of wealth redistribution is of particular relevance to the lives of RIT students. The senator has repeatedly stood against the coercive forces of surveillance capitalism that plague students from all backgrounds. Graduating students are left vulnerable when they enter a labor market that is anti-union and queer-opposed. Sanders and his socialist contemporaries offer up a truly democratic alternative to capitalism's systemic power inequalities. With a history of direct action against discriminatory wealth policing, Sanders sets himself apart from presidential candidates that do little but propose ineffectual tweaks to the status quo. RIT YDSA members have highlighted Medicare for All, pacifist international relations, and internet-as-utility legislation as actionable Sanders-led proposals set to minimize capitalistic injustice both within the United States and abroad.
